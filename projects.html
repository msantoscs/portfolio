Rehospitalization Project

This project is about reducing rehospitalization rate of post-acute pateints. These are focused initiatives to prevent patients from goinf back to the hospital which are as follows:

1. Initiation of care within 24-48hours from the date referral or date of discharge.

2. Front loading of visits within five days in service. At least 3 touched points.

3. Provider follow-up appointment within the week post discharge.

4. Additional discipline evaluation needs to be done within the 5 day window.
